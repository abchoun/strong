  • Son Goku

    • Goku is a legendary and iconic character in the world of anime and manga, created by Akira Toriyama. He is the central figure in the Dragon Ball series, a franchise that has captivated generations of fans worldwide. Goku's journey begins as a young and naive boy with a monkey tail, living in the mountains. Over the course of the series, he transforms into a powerful warrior and martial artist, with a pure heart and an insatiable appetite for self-improvement. Goku is known for his signature orange gi, spiky black hair, and his trademark move, the Kamehameha, a powerful energy blast. He is not only a formidable fighter but also a symbol of perseverance and the unyielding spirit of never giving up, even in the face of overwhelming odds. His adventures, from the original Dragon Ball series to Dragon Ball Z and beyond, have left an indelible mark on pop culture, making Son Goku a beloved and enduring character cherished by fans of all ages , and his current and stronger form is The Ultra Instinct
